How to Use LeadsForge

  1. Open Google Maps in Chrome.
  2. Search for a business type and city, or enter multiple searches in the Locations field.
  3. Click the LeadsForge extension icon.
  4. Set your filters (website preference, min rating, exclude closed).
  5. Enable Deep Scan for social media links and detailed info.
  6. Click Start Scan and wait for completion.
  7. Select the businesses you want to export using checkboxes.
  8. Export to Excel, CSV, or Google Sheets.
